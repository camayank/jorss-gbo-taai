# CPA Partnership Manager
**Commission-Based · Remote · Flexible Hours**

## What You're Selling

CA4CPA Global LLC has built a white-label AI tax advisor that CPA firms give to clients for free.

**The CPA firm gets:**
- A branded portal (e.g., `smith-cpa.advisor.ca4cpa.com`)
- Every client that completes the advisor sends the CPA a fully filled-out tax profile — filing status, income, deductions, retirement, real estate, all of it
- A review dashboard showing every session, identified strategies, and estimated savings
- No cost to the firm

**The client gets:**
- A free, personalized tax analysis
- Education on 30+ strategies they may qualify for
- A path to book a consultation with the CPA

**You get:**
- **$500** per CPA firm fully onboarded (at least one client completes the advisor)
- **$50/month** per active firm (recurring, as long as they're using it)
- 10 firms = $500 onboarding + $500/month ongoing from day one

## What Onboarding Looks Like

1. **30-min demo call** — you show them the live advisor and their branded dashboard
2. **Setup** — we configure their branded subdomain in 24 hours
3. **First client** — they share the link with 5 clients via email
4. **First review** — you walk them through their first client session together
5. **Done** — they're onboarded, you earned $500

Total time per firm: 3–5 hours including follow-up.

## Target Firms

- Independent CPA practices and small tax firms (2–10 person shops)
- Annual revenue $500K–$5M
- Already doing individual tax returns (not just corporate/audit)
- Ideally: firms that feel squeezed by TurboTax and H&R Block taking their simple clients

**Best niches to start:** Real estate CPAs, self-employment/freelancer specialists, high-income individual tax practices.

## This Is NOT a Technical Role

You don't need to understand the AI or the tax engine. You need to:
- Have relationships with CPA firms or know how to build them quickly
- Be comfortable doing short demos over Zoom
- Follow up consistently (most deals close on the 2nd or 3rd contact)
- Speak the language of a tax professional (you don't need to be one)

## Ideal Background

- Former CPA, tax preparer, or tax software sales (Lacerte, Drake, UltraTax)
- Sold to professional services firms (insurance, payroll, HR software)
- Strong network in the accounting community
- Or: ambitious self-starter willing to cold-call/email 50 firms in month one

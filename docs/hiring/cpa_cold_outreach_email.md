Use these to reach CPA firm owners directly. Personalize the first line.
Send from a personal email, not a marketing platform — these read better 1:1.

---

## Notes on sending

- **Best days:** Tuesday–Thursday, 7–9am or 11am–1pm recipient's timezone
- **Best months to reach CPAs:** June–September (post-filing, pre-planning season)
- **Avoid:** January–April (tax season), first week of any month
- **Volume:** Send 10–15 per day, personalize first line, track reply rate
- **Target reply rate:** 8–15% for Version B (pain-point) to warm intros
- **Goal:** Book 2–3 demos per week

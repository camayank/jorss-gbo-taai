## Executive Summary

**Purpose**: This document defines the computation report format that serves as the centerpiece of our tax advisory platform - transforming basic compliance into strategic tax planning.

**Key Principle**: Every computation must be:
1. **Transparent** - Show the math, not just results
2. **Actionable** - Lead to specific recommendations
3. **Scenario-based** - Compare alternatives with dollar impact
4. **Multi-year aware** - Project future implications
5. **Professionally formatted** - Match expectations of 25-year CPA veterans

## Key Messaging Framework

### Primary Value Proposition
**"Stop competing on price. Start winning on value."**

### Three Pillars

| Pillar | Message | Feature |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **Speed** | Answer "what if" in seconds, not days | Delta Analysis |
| **Revenue** | Turn $300 prep into $1,500 advisory | Advisory Reports |
| **Focus** | Stop chasing tire-kickers | Lead Scoring |

- 40% increase in advisory revenue (case study)
- 45 seconds to generate advisory reports
- 1,557 passing tests for accuracy
- 40+ IRS forms supported

## Target Audiences

### Primary: Progressive CPA Firms
- 2-20 employees
- Focus on small business clients
- Want to grow advisory revenue
- Open to new technology

### Secondary: Solo Practitioners
- High expertise, limited time
- Complex client base
- Need efficiency gains
- Price-sensitive but ROI-focused

### Tertiary: Enterprise Firms
- 20+ employees
- White-label requirements
- Custom integration needs
- Budget available for premium

## Campaign Calendar (Suggested)

### Pre-Tax Season (Oct-Dec)
- Focus: "Get ready for tax season"
- Emphasis: Learning curve, workflow integration
- Offer: Extended trial periods

- Emphasis: Time savings, efficiency

## A/B Testing Priorities

### Headlines to Test
1. "Stop Competing on Price" vs "Grow Advisory Revenue 40%"
2. "Real-Time Tax Analysis" vs "Answer What-If Questions Instantly"
3. "For CPAs" vs "For Tax Professionals"

### CTAs to Test
1. "Start Free Trial" vs "See It In Action"
2. "Book a Demo" vs "Watch 2-Minute Video"
3. "Get Started" vs "Try Free for 14 Days"

### Offers to Test
1. 14-day trial vs 30-day trial
2. Credit card required vs no credit card
3. Discount code vs no discount

## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Tone
- Professional but not stuffy
- Confident but not arrogant
- Direct and clear
- Results-focused

### Avoid
- Jargon and buzzwords
- Overpromising
- Negative competitor mentions
- Technical complexity in marketing copy

### Use
- Specific numbers and metrics
- Real client examples (with permission)
- Clear before/after comparisons
- Questions that prompt reflection

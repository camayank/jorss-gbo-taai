1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you use the site.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings menu.
  • Cookie Banner: When you first visit our site, you can accept or decline non-essential cookies.
  • Opt-out Links: For analytics cookies, you can opt out using Google's opt-out tool.

Note: Blocking essential cookies may prevent you from using certain features of our Service, including login functionality.

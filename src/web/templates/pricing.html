Frequently Asked Questions

How is my tax data handled? ▼

Your tax information is protected with bank-level encryption (AES-256). We comply with all federal tax privacy regulations and never sell your data. Our servers are hosted in secure, HIPAA-compliant infrastructure. For CPA white-label users, you retain full control over client data.

Can I upgrade or downgrade plans? ▼

Yes. You can upgrade to Premium Advisory at any time. Downgrading is also available. Changes take effect immediately, and you'll be prorated for any differences in your current billing period.

Is this CPA-licensed tax advice? ▼

The Free Estimate provides AI-generated planning ideas, not formal tax advice. Premium Advisory consultations are conducted by licensed CPAs. See IRS Circular 230 for details. All advice is for planning purposes only; consult your tax professional for your specific situation.

What about white-label customization? ▼

CPA white-label plans include full branding, custom domain setup, API access for integration with your existing systems, and dedicated onboarding support. Contact our sales team for a custom quote based on your practice size and feature needs.

Do you offer refunds? ▼

The Free Estimate requires no payment. Premium Advisory consultations are non-refundable after the consultation is completed. CPA white-label plans operate on monthly subscriptions with a 30-day cancellation notice.

## Document Upload Instructions

---

### How to Upload Your Tax Documents

**Step 1: Log In**
Go to {{PORTAL_URL}} and sign in with your email and password.

**Step 2: Navigate to Documents**
Click "Documents" in the left menu, then "Upload New Document."

**Step 3: Select Document Type**
Choose the category that matches your document:
- W-2
- 1099
- 1098
- K-1
- Other

**Step 4: Upload**
Drag and drop your file, or click to browse. We accept PDF, JPG, and PNG files up to 10MB.

**Step 5: Confirm**
Add any notes (optional) and click "Upload." You'll see a confirmation when it's complete.

**Tips:**
- Name files clearly (e.g., "W2_Employer_Name_2025.pdf")
- Upload as documents arriveâ€”don't wait for everything
- Check the "Missing Documents" section to see what we still need

---

### Scope of Services

We will prepare the following tax return(s) based on information you provide:

- [ ] Federal Individual Income Tax Return (Form 1040)
- [ ] State Income Tax Return: {{STATE}}
- [ ] Additional State Return: {{STATE_2}} (if applicable)

**Our services include:**
- Preparation of the above return(s) for the tax year ending December 31, {{TAX_YEAR}}
- Identification of relevant deductions and credits based on information provided
- Electronic filing of your return(s) upon your approval
- Response to notices related to the return(s) we prepare (first year only)

**Our services do NOT include:**
- Audit representation (available separately)
- Tax planning for future years (available separately)
- Bookkeeping or accounting services
- Amended returns for prior years
- Review of returns not prepared by our firm

---

### Your Responsibilities

To ensure accurate and complete tax returns, you agree to:

1. **Provide complete and accurate information** including all Forms W-2, 1099, K-1, and other tax documents

2. **Respond promptly** to our requests for additional information or clarification

3. **Review the completed return** before signing and authorizing e-file

4. **Retain copies** of all tax documents for at least seven (7) years

5. **Notify us** of any changes to your tax situation that occur before filing

You are responsible for the accuracy and completeness of the information you provide. We will not audit or otherwise verify the data you submit, although we may ask for clarification or additional documentation.

---

### Fees and Payment

**Service Fee:** ${{SERVICE_FEE}}

This fee covers the preparation of the return(s) listed above. Additional fees may apply for:
- Additional state returns: ${{STATE_FEE}} each
- Schedule C (business income): ${{SCHEDULE_C_FEE}}
- Schedule E (rental income): ${{SCHEDULE_E_FEE}}
- Schedule D with multiple transactions: ${{SCHEDULE_D_FEE}}
- Complex situations requiring additional time: ${{HOURLY_RATE}}/hour

**Payment Terms:**
- 50% deposit due upon signing this engagement letter
- Remaining balance due before e-filing
- We accept: Check, Credit Card, ACH Transfer

---

### Tax Return Delivery

Upon completion, you will receive:
- PDF copy of your federal and state return(s)
- Filing confirmation after IRS acceptance
- Access to your return through our secure client portal

Returns will be retained electronically for seven (7) years.

---

### Disclaimers and Limitations

1. **Estimates and Projections:** Any estimates or projections provided during this engagement are based on assumptions that may change. Actual results may differ.

2. **Tax Law Changes:** Tax laws are subject to change. We will prepare your return based on laws in effect at the time of filing.

3. **IRS Examination:** Our engagement does not include representation in the event of an IRS examination. Such services are available under a separate engagement.

4. **Reliance on Information:** We will rely on the information you provide without independent verification. You remain legally responsible for the accuracy of your tax return.

---

By signing below, you:

1. Authorize us to prepare and electronically file your {{TAX_YEAR}} tax return(s)
2. Acknowledge that you have read and understand this engagement letter
3. Agree to the fees and payment terms described above
4. Confirm that you will provide complete and accurate information

- Preparation or filing of tax returns (available separately)
- Representation before the IRS
- Legal advice (we will refer you to an attorney if needed)
- Ongoing monitoring unless separately engaged
- Implementation of recommendations (we advise; you decide and act)

---

### Your Responsibilities

You agree to:

1. Provide complete and accurate financial information
2. Disclose all relevant facts about your tax situation
3. Respond to information requests within {{RESPONSE_DAYS}} business days
4. Make your own decisions about whether to implement recommendations
5. Consult with other professionals (attorneys, financial advisors) as appropriate

---

### Fees and Payment

**Advisory Fee:** ${{ADVISORY_FEE}}

**What's Included:**
- Initial consultation
- Analysis and report preparation
- Written advisory report
- {{CONSULTATION_HOURS}}-hour follow-up consultation

**Additional Services (if requested):**
- Additional consultation: ${{HOURLY_RATE}}/hour
- Revised analysis (significant changes): ${{REVISION_FEE}}
- Implementation assistance: Quoted separately

**Payment Terms:**
- Full payment due upon signing this engagement letter
- OR 50% deposit, 50% upon delivery of advisory report
- Payment methods: Check, Credit Card, ACH Transfer

---

### Important Disclaimers

**1. Advisory Nature of Services**

The recommendations in our advisory report are based on current tax law and the information you provide. Tax laws change, and your circumstances may differ from our assumptions. Our recommendations are advisory only—you are responsible for deciding whether and how to implement them.

**2. No Guarantee of Results**

We cannot guarantee specific tax savings or outcomes. Actual results depend on many factors beyond our control, including changes in tax law, IRS interpretation, and the accuracy of information provided.

**3. Not Legal Advice**

Our services do not include legal advice. If legal issues arise, we will recommend you consult with a qualified attorney.

**4. Reliance on Information Provided**

We will rely on the information you provide without independent verification. The quality of our advice depends on the completeness and accuracy of your information.

**5. Point-in-Time Analysis**

Our analysis reflects your situation and applicable tax law at a specific point in time. We recommend reviewing your tax strategy periodically, especially when major life changes occur.

---

### Confidentiality

We will maintain the confidentiality of all information you provide, except as required by law or professional standards. We will not disclose your information to third parties without your written consent.

---

By signing below, you:

1. Engage {{FIRM_NAME}} to provide tax advisory services as described
2. Acknowledge the limitations and disclaimers stated above
3. Agree to the fees and payment terms
4. Authorize us to request information from third parties on your behalf (if needed)

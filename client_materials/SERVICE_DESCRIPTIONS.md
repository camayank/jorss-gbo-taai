## How Our Pricing Works

---

### Transparent Pricing

We believe in transparent, upfront pricing:

1. **No surprises** — We provide a fee estimate before we begin work
2. **Scope-based** — Fees are based on complexity, not hours
3. **All-inclusive** — Quoted prices include everything described
4. **Fair adjustments** — If your situation is simpler than expected, we'll adjust

### How We Determine Complexity

We consider:
- Number and type of income sources
- Business activities
- Investment activity
- Deduction complexity
- Special situations (foreign, multi-state, etc.)

### What's NOT Included in Standard Pricing

- Prior year returns (quoted separately)
- Multiple states (add-on pricing)
- IRS audit representation (separate engagement)
- Legal advice (we'll refer you to an attorney)
- Bookkeeping/accounting (separate service)
